Understanding Skincare Ingredients

Published on November 25, 2025 | Ingredients

Navigating active ingredients can be confusing. Here is a quick guide to some of the most effective ingredients found in Beauty Times products:

Hyaluronic Acid (Hydrator)

This molecule can hold up to 1,000 times its weight in water, drawing moisture into the skin. Itâ€™s ideal for **Face Serums** and creams for plumping and reducing fine lines.

Vitamin C (Brightener)

A powerful antioxidant that combats free radicals, brightens dark spots (hyperpigmentation), and boosts collagen production. Look for this in **Face Serums** and **Face Creams**.

Salicylic Acid (BHA - Exfoliator)

This oil-soluble acid penetrates pores to dissolve buildup and fight acne. It is often found in **Cleansers** and targeted toners for oily or combination skin.
