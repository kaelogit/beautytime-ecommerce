Build Your Perfect Skincare Routine

Published on November 25, 2025 | Routine Guide

The secret to glowing skin isn't finding one miracle product, but establishing a routine that works consistently. Products must be layered correctly to ensure maximum absorption and effectiveness.

The Essential 4-Step Regimen

  1. **Cleanse:** Use a Face Wash or Cleanser to remove makeup, dirt, and excess oil. This is non-negotiable, morning and night.
  2. **Tone/Treat:** Apply a Toner or targeted treatment (like a Face Serum or a Vitamin C product). This step prepares your skin for hydration and delivers active ingredients.
  3. **Moisturize:** Apply your Face Cream or Body Cream. This step locks in moisture and strengthens the skin barrier.
  4. **Protect (AM Only):** Always finish your morning routine with a Face Sunscreen.

Layering Tip: Thin to Thick

Always apply products from the thinnest consistency (toners/serums) to the thickest (creams/oils). This ensures lighter formulas can penetrate before being blocked by richer ones.
