Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Beauty Times ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (the "Site").

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you place an order, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us.

  • **Identity & Contact Data:** Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address (collected during checkout).
  • **Transaction Data:** Details about payments and products purchased (handled securely via payment gateway APIs).
  • **Usage Data:** Information about how you use our Site, such as your IP address, browser type, and time spent on pages (collected automatically via browser tools).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders (the primary purpose of collecting shipping details).
  • Communicate with you regarding your order status and customer service inquiries.
  • Send marketing emails if you have opted in (e.g., our newsletter).

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with third parties necessary to operate our business:

  • **Shipping Partners:** To deliver your purchases.
  • **Payment Processors:** To process secure transactions (e.g., Paystack/Flutterwave).

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: hello@beautytimes.com.
